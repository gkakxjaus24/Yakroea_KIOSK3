I want to have my laundry done.
Place all laundry into one of the blue bags provided on the reception counter (5,000 won. Both washing/drying are included). Place the laundry fee in the bag on the right. Laundry will be completed within 24 hours and can be collected from in front of the reception counter.
When does the main door close?
The main door is open 24hrs a day.
Where is the drinking water?
Please use the water purifier on the counter in the kitchen. (For hot water, use the electric kettle)
I need more towels.
First, place all used towels(s) in the 'used towel' basket and then get new towels from the basket on the reception counter.
What is the Wi-Fi?
In every room above the light switch or on the wall, the Wi-Fi ID and password are written down.
When is the breakfast ready?
Breakfast is from 7am-10pm and is self-service.
Why is there no one at the reception counter?
In order to provide our guests with the best possible prices, we've taken personnel costs into account and are operating without reception staff. We appreciate your understanding. However, if help is needed, feel free to call the number provided.
I need to borrow an adaptor.
Adaptors and phone chargers are provided in the box on the reception counter. Please return them when checking out.
Is there a public restroom?
Unfortunately, there is no pulbic restroom on the 1st floor.
How long may I use the common area for?
The common area can be used 24 hrs a day, however there are other guest rooms on the first floor. Please try to be quiet after 11pm.
I would like to store my luggage.
Before/After check in, luggage can be stored under the table across from the reception counter.
Is there any way to weigh my luggage?
On the table across from the counter, there is a scale that can be used to weigh luggage.
I need further assistance.
Please use the phone on the counter to contact the provided number.
